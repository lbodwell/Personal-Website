Relatable DAW is an online, collaborative, interactive digital audio workstation developed with the MERN stack. Its goal is to teach music theory concepts through relation-based composition. Audio synthesis is handled with Tone.js, and the combination of a NoSQL database and web sockets allow users to save, share, and collaborate simultaneously on projects after signing in via Google OAuth. This project was developed and iterated on based on the insights of a professional high school band director.

GeoBlade is a 3D third-person action-adventure game currently in development using Unity, in which audio and music play a major design role. The game features a rich location-driven narrative complete with original music, fully voice-acted dialogue, and dynamic sound effects implemented in Audiokinetic Wwise. Players will face challenges in the form of both combat and puzzles as they meet friends, foes, and those in between while unravelling the truth of this mysterious world.
